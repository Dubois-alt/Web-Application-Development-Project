Who we are

Born from a love of Kenyan coffee

Barcelona House opened in 2024 inspired by the warm, neighbourhood cafés of Spain's old town. We wanted to bring that same unhurried energy to Westlands — a place where the espresso is taken seriously and you're always welcome to stay for one more cup.

Our team is led by head barista Amina Wanjiku, who trained in Melbourne and Amsterdam before returning home to lead our coffee program.
