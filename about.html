Our story

About Barcelona House

Barcelona House was founded in 2024 with a passion that goes beyond coffee. Inspired by the world-famous football club FC Barcelona, our café was created to reflect the club's values of excellence, unity, passion, and community. Just as FC Barcelona brings people together through the beautiful game, we aim to bring people together over exceptional coffee, delicious food, and meaningful conversations.

Located in the heart of Westlands, Barcelona House combines the excitement and spirit of Barcelona with the warmth of Kenyan hospitality. From our carefully selected coffee beans to our welcoming atmosphere, every detail is designed to celebrate the pride, culture, and winning mentality that have made Barcelona one of the most admired football clubs in the world. Whether you're watching a match with friends or enjoying a quiet cup of coffee, Barcelona House is a place where everyone feels like part of the team.
