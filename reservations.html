Reservation Policy

📅 Booking window

Reservations can be made up to 30 days in advance and at least 2 hours before your desired time.

👥 Group bookings

For parties of more than 8, please contact us directly at hello@barcelonahouse.co.ke so we can make suitable arrangements.

⏰ Table hold time

We hold reserved tables for 15 minutes. If you're running late, please call us to avoid losing your spot.

❌ Cancellations

Please cancel at least 2 hours in advance so we can open the table to other guests. You can reach us at +254 712 345 678.
